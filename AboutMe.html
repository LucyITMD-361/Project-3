About Me

My interest for coding all started a couple of years ago, I was bored and decided to google, fun things to do when bored. While on my search, someone mentioned to make a website. That caught my interest and I became curious as to how exactly a website is made. Through my research, I came across the object-oriented language, HTML and CSS. From there, I found the website, CodeAcadamy. If you don't know about CodeAcadamy I suggest you check the website out. Once, I learned the basics and saw my output come to life, I was amazed at what I had created.

Now, thinking back I'm glad that I googled fun things to do when bored. Since, then I have been coding for fun. My hobbies are to code on CodeAcadamy or freeCodeCamp, researching a topic of interest, and exercise. I use many apps to code but my two main sites are the ones mentioned previously. Researching a topic of interest is one of my favorite things to do. The duration of my research last about a month. Some topics that I have conducted research on are finances, inspiration, self-improvement, and business. I enjoy seeking knowledge. I feel that once I graduate I will continue to seek knowledge until the end of my time. During my busy schedule, I try to squeeze in a workout 5 days a week. I enjoy exercising as it relieves stress.
